DIRECT SHEAR TEST ON MILD STEEL PLATE

Objective

To determine experimentally, the ultimate shear strength in double shear of mild steel plate.

Apparatus used:

Universal testing machine, Shear attachments,Vernier calipers.

STEP  1 

 Take the mild steel plate specimen and determine the width 'w' & thickness 'd' of the mild steel plate using vernier calipers.

Mild Steel Plate

STEP    2 

 The mild steel plate is placed in the shear attachment and placed into the Universal Testing machine.

STEP    3 

 The machine is switched ON & the load is gradually increased until the specimen is fractured and the required load is noted down.

STEP   4 

 Calculate the double shear strength of mild steel plate.
